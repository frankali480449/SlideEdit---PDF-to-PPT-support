PDF to PPT Converter

About

SlideEdit is a powerful tool that converts PDF files to editable PowerPoint presentations. All processing is done locally on your device, ensuring your documents remain private and secure.

Features

  • Convert PDF to editable PPT
  • Separate text and images automatically
  • Edit text directly in the app
  • Save presentations locally
  • 100% offline processing

How to Use

1. Open the app and tap "Import PDF"

2. Select your PDF file

3. Wait for the conversion to complete

4. Edit text and images as needed

5. Save your PPT file

FAQ

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes! All files are processed locally on your device. We never upload your documents to any server.

Q: What PDF formats are supported?
A: SlideEdit supports standard PDF files. For best results, use PDFs with clear text and images.
