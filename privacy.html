Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SlideEdit ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Summary: SlideEdit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All file processing happens locally on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

SlideEdit is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Your PDF or PPT files

Local Processing

All PDF to PPT conversion is performed entirely on your device. Your documents are never uploaded to any external server. This means:

  • Your files stay on your device
  • No internet connection is required for conversion
  • Your documents remain completely private

Third-Party Services

SlideEdit uses OpenCV, an open-source computer vision library, for image processing. OpenCV runs locally on your device and does not collect or transmit any data.

Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to secure on our end. Your files are protected by your device's built-in security features.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
